Forest Loss

  1. To start the forest loss analysis, click the Indicators button to open the indicators dialog.

  1. On the indicators modal click the Forest button to open the forest indicators dialog

  1. On the indicators modal click the Forest Loss button to open the forest loss dialog

  1. Provide all the required details on the dialog and click submit.

  1. Visualize statistics on the dashboard by clicking the Statistics button .

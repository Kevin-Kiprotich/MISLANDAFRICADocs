Water Erosion

  1. To start the Water Erosion analysis, click the Indicators button to open the indicators dialog.

  1. Click the Soil Erosion button to open the Soil Erosion dialog.

  1. Click the Water Erosion button to open the Water Erosion dialog.

  1. Provide all the required details on the dialog and click submit.

  2. Visualize statistics on the dashboard by clicking the Statistics button .
